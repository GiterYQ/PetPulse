AI Design

AI doesn't replace help.
It makes it faster.

🎯
Smart Alert Generation
Auto-generates emergency broadcasts with correct blood type, urgency level and location context
🔍
Pet Feature Recognition
CLIP-based image matching identifies breed, color, and physical traits from a photo
🩸
Blood Donor Matching
Semantic matching ranks available donors by compatibility, proximity, and historical responsiveness
📡
Network Coordination
Orchestrates multi-party dispatch — from alert to confirmation — without human bottleneck
AI = Amplifier, not Actor.
